— The dentist

Three decades, quietly building one of Bengaluru's most-trusted dental practices.

Dr. H. L. Jayakumar trained at Government Dental College, Bangalore, completed his MDS in Pedodontics, and has spent the last 30+ years in practice on Modi Hospital Road. He guides PhD candidates at Rajiv Gandhi Health University and lectures at international dental conferences.

His patients tend to describe the same two things: that the procedure was explained before it began, and that the cost was understood before they sat in the chair.
