Common Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

Modern root canal treatment is virtually painless. We use advanced anaesthesia techniques and precision instruments to ensure your complete comfort throughout the procedure. Most patients compare the sensation to getting a simple filling.
With proper care and maintenance, dental implants can last a lifetime. The implant itself is made of titanium, which fuses with your jawbone. Regular check-ups and good oral hygiene are key to longevity.
We recommend a child's first dental visit by their first birthday or within six months of the first tooth appearing. Early visits help establish good dental habits and allow us to monitor development.
The cost of braces varies depending on the type (metal, ceramic, or clear aligners) and the complexity of the case. We provide transparent pricing during your consultation and offer flexible payment plans to make treatment accessible.
Yes, we provide same-day emergency dental care. If you're experiencing severe pain, a broken tooth, or any urgent dental issue, call us immediately or reach out on WhatsApp for priority scheduling.
We maintain the highest standards of infection control with 100% sterilization protocol, including Class B autoclaving, UV sanitization, and strict PPE usage. Every surface is disinfected between patients for your safety.
